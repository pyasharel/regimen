### Google Play Optimization Tips

1. **Full description is SEO-critical** - Unlike iOS, every word in the full description is indexed by Google Play
2. **Natural keyword integration** - Include target keywords naturally throughout the description

## 11. Strategic Insights

### Why "Log" Keywords Are Golden
- Lower competition than "tracker" keywords
- Same user intent (people want to track)
- Compounds well with drug names (testosterone log, injection log)

### Why Reconstitution Calculator Is Best Opportunity
- Highest traffic (46) of untapped keywords
- Medium difficulty (40) = achievable
- Calculator is already in subtitle
- Content marketing can boost organic ranking

### Conversion Insights
- TRT/power peptide users convert 3x better than single-compound GLP-1 users
- Focus ASO on high-intent "log" and "protocol" terms
- De-prioritize brand keywords (Ozempic, Mounjaro) - low conversion
